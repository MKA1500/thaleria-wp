Relocation Support


We provide all the help you will ever need for a smooth relocation. This includes detailed guides, help finding an apartment, family support and extensive advice on administrative, legal and fiscal processes. If you are not a citizen of the European Union, we will also sponsor, help and guide you through the entire process of obtaining a work permit to work in Belgium.


After Care

We offer continuous support beyond the relocation because we understand that it’s hard to live abroad – and it’s nice to have someone on whom you can rely. We have someone available for you 24/7 and we organize regular fun networking events to help you meet people in a similar situation.

Career Development


We work with different service providers as well as our clients to make relevant trainings available to you and help you grow in your field. In the long run, we also establish an industry career plan with you to make sure your career is going in the direction you want it to.
